# Wardrobe taxonomy

## Categories and subcategories

- `top`: `tshirt`, `shirt`, `knitwear`, `hoodie`, `jacket`, `other`
- `bottom`: `trousers`, `shorts`, `swimwear`, `skirt`, `other`
- `shoes`: `sneakers`, `leather_shoes`, `boots`, `sandals`, `other`
- `bag`: `handbag`, `shoulder_bag`, `backpack`, `other`

## Colors

`black`, `white`, `gray`, `beige`, `brown`, `navy`, `blue`, `green`, `red`, `pink`, `purple`, `yellow`, `orange`, `metallic`, `multicolor`, `unknown`

## Seasons

`spring`, `summer`, `autumn`, `winter`, `all_season`

Use `other` or `unknown` rather than inventing values. For swimming trunks use `bottom` + `swimwear`.
